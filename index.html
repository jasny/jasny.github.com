LegalThings

LegalThings is an application aimed at digitializing paper intensive industries like lawyer firms, accounting firms and HR departments. With LegalThings you can control the full document life cycle online; purchasing legal templates, creating documents, collaboration, reviewing, signing and storage.

Arnold is the architect of this project. Starting with a minimal viable product, this project quickly gained traction. Jasny continued developing the application into a highly scalable robust application, while LegalThings setup an internal team. The app now effortly serves thousands of professionals and hundreds of thousands of clients.

  • PHP
  • Node.js
  • AngularJS
  • AWS
  • Continues integration

MyRadio – Station manager

MyRadio is interactive internet radio, owned by Netherlands largest radio company Sky Radio Group. MyRadio offered 30 stations, each carefully created by music directors. While listening to the station, you can tell which songs you like or dislike causing the station to gradually change according to your taste.

Jasny has created the backend application which allows music directors to upload and process new tracks and the sales department to schedule ads. It’s also processes the logs from a million tracks played each day, to generate insights and reporting.

MyRadio has been sold to a third party and discontinued as standalone application.

  • PHP
  • MySQL
  • Zend Framework
  • jQuery
  • Bootstrap

10.000scholen – Manager

The website 10.000scholen.nl provides detailed information about all primary and secondary schools in the Netherlands. This includesstatistical data like the performance of the school, age and gender of the teacher and education level of the parents.

Several government agencies each release part of the data, all in non-standard format. The manager imports and processes these files and combines it with existing data.
